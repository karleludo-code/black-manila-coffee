BLACK + MANILA: Breakfast Blend

A special blend of Liberica coffee from regions in Batangas and Cavite, combined with Arabica from regions of Benguet. This blend combines bold and sweet flavor of the famed Kapeng Barako with the unique sweetness and acidity from Arabica varietals found in Benguet.

Black Manila's Breakfast blend combines qualities from both beans to create the perfectly balanced cup, perfect coffee to start the day.

Ingredients:
30% Arabica, 70% Robusta
Roast:
Medium Dark Roast
Flavor:
Bold and bittersweet flavor with smooth mouthfeel. With Notes of Nuts, chocolates and caramel
Complimentary Food Flavors:
Nuts, chocolates and caramel
